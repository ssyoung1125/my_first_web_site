Every letter I sent you.
This album reflects memories, dilemmas, and thought processes I had from when I was nineteen to twenty three.
With no specific address, I've been mailing my feelings in form of songs.
And I'm really glad that I can finally mail them to you all.
This album is my way of thanking everyone who has been loving me and supporting my journey as a musician.
